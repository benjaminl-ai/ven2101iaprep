1. Greeting & Intro

👋 Start with a warm welcome and introduce yourself.
Useful Expressions (Click to use):
  • "Good morning, everybody!..."
  • "Hello, everybody! Welcome..."

2. Purpose

🎯 Tell them what you are introducing today.
  • "Today, I’m going to introduce..."
  • "May I take this opportunity..."

3. Details

ℹ️ Launch date, price, and how to get more info.
enrolvisitwebsitefee
  • "With effect from (Date)..."
  • "In this app / store, you will..."

4. Special Offer

🎁 What can the audience get if they sign up?
  • "If you enrol, we would be happy to..."
  • "Our company is pleased to offer..."

5. Invitation

✉️ Invite them to a trial, taster or visit.
  • "I’d like to invite you to..."
  • "You’re invited to..."

6. Closing

🏁 End the briefing and ask for questions.
  • "That’s the end... Are there any questions?"
  • "Thank you for your attention..."
